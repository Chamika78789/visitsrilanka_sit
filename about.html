About Sri Lanka

The Pearl of the Indian Ocean

Welcome to Paradise

Sri Lanka, officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country in South Asia. It lies in the Indian Ocean, southwest of the Bay of Bengal, and southeast of the Arabian Sea.

Known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean", Sri Lanka is a land of incredible beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality. From pristine beaches to misty mountains, ancient temples to colonial architecture, this island nation offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

With over 2,500 years of recorded history, eight UNESCO World Heritage Sites, diverse wildlife, and world-famous Ceylon tea, Sri Lanka truly has something for everyone.
